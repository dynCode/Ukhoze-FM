Introduction:

Welcome to Mahala Loyalty Programme’s privacy policy. We are Mahala Loyalty Programme (Pty) Ltd (Registration number 2001/030145/07) and all of its direct or indirect subsidiaries and affiliates. Mahala Loyalty Programme respect your privacy and take the protection of personal information very seriously. The purpose of this policy is to describe the way that we collect, store, use, and protect information that can be associated with you or another specific natural or juristic person and can be used to identify you or that person (personal information).

Audience:

This policy applies to you if you are:

  • a visitor to our website
  • submitted online applications | online contact Requests forms; or
  • a customer who has requested the services that we provide.
  • radio listener and participate in radio promotions
  • a Mahala member
This policy applies to you under certain circumstances.

Use of your personal information:

Mahala Loyalty Programme is committed to keeping your personal information confidential. Mahala Loyalty Programme will use your personal information to enhance your experience, the service that Mahala Loyalty Programme provides to you and to communicate with you, unless expressly directed by you not to do so.

  • Mahala collects your personal information for the following reasons:
  • Name & Surname - to identify you on the database
  • Identity number - unique identifier to assure we have no duplicates on our system
  • Gender - to match applicable deals that are relevant to you
  • Contact Numbers - to inform you of new deals available to you
  • Address - to assist Mahala to negotiate deals within your region
  • E-mail address - to inform you of new deals available to you
  • Bank details – for debit orders and collection of fees

Mahala will only communicate information relevant to Mahala Loyalty Programme and/or your benefits applied for within the prescribed format when you sign-up.

  • Messages and updates
    We may send administrative messages and email updates to you about the website, applications and services. In some cases, we may also send you primarily promotional messages. We may wish to provide you with information about new services in which we think you may be interested. We will not send you promotional messages unless you have chosen to opt-into them. But, we may send you one message asking you to opt-into promotional messages without you having opted-into promotional messages.
  • Targeted content
    While you are logged into the website | Mahala App | Mahala Mobi Site, we may display targeted adverts and other relevant information based on your personal information. In a completely automated process, computers process the personal information and match it to adverts or related information. We never share personal information with any advertiser, unless you specifically provide us with your consent to do so. Advertisers receive a record of the total number of impressions and clicks for each advert. They do not receive any personal information. If you click on an advert, we may send a referring URL to the advertiser’s website identifying that a customer is visiting from the website or application. We do not send personal information to advertisers with the referring URL. Once you are on the advertiser’s website however, the advertiser is able to collect your personal information.
    We may use your information for targeted content in certain, specified instances.

For more information, please view our Data Security Description.

If requested, you may choose to give us your personal information, for example your name and address or email addresses.
